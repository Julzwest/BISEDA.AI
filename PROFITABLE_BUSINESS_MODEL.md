#### PRO PLAN - $14.99/month ‚≠ê MAIN PRODUCT
- **Messages:** 200 per day
- **Features:** Everything + Image analysis (20/day)
- **Cost to you:** ~$1.80/month
- **Profit:** $14.99 - $1.80 = **$13.19/month** (88% margin)
