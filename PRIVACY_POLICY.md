# Privacy Policy - Biseda.ai

**Last Updated:** November 26, 2025

## Introduction

Biseda.ai ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## Age Restriction

**This application is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older.** By using this application, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Age Verification Data**: We collect your date of birth to verify you are 18 years or older. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
- **Chat Messages**: When you use the chat features, your messages are processed to generate AI responses. Messages may be temporarily stored in your device's local storage.
- **Images**: If you upload images (e.g., for style advice), these images are processed by our AI service. Images are converted to base64 format and sent to our backend API for analysis.

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system, and device identifiers.
- **Usage Data**: We may collect information about how you interact with the application, including features used and time spent in the app.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Services**: Process your chat messages and generate AI responses
2. **Image Analysis**: Analyze uploaded images to provide style advice
3. **Age Verification**: Verify that users are 18 years or older
4. **Improve Services**: Analyze usage patterns to improve our application
5. **Security**: Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI API

We use OpenAI's API to power our AI chat features. When you send messages:

- Your messages are sent to OpenAI's servers for processing
- OpenAI's privacy policy applies to data sent to their services
- We do not control OpenAI's data practices
- For more information, visit: https://openai.com/privacy

### Data Transmission

- Chat messages are sent to our backend server, which then forwards them to OpenAI
- Images are converted to base64 format and sent through our backend to OpenAI
- We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

- Age verification status is stored locally on your device
- Chat history may be stored locally on your device
- This data is not transmitted to our servers

### Server Storage

- We do not permanently store your chat messages on our servers
- Messages are temporarily processed and then discarded
- We may log API usage statistics (without personal identifiers)

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- **Secure Backend**: API keys and sensitive data are stored server-side only
- **Rate Limiting**: We implement rate limiting to prevent abuse
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to data on a need-to-know basis

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
